The Core Idea

One Word. One Chance to Think Differently.

We took the whole word-guessing thing and basically stripped out all the annoying parts that don't actually make the game better.
No clutter, no distractions, no pressure to keep playing endlessly. Each day gives you a new word. That’s it.
You can approach it however you want: quickly, instinctively, or slowly, thoughtfully. There’s no “correct” pace — only your way of solving it.
