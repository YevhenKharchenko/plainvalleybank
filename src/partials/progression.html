Rescue Missions

Every Level Has a Purpose

Wind Down isn’t limited to one moment per day. When you feel like playing more, there’s always something waiting.

  • Rescue Baby Turtles

    Clear obstacles and open safe routes so baby turtles can reach the water and continue their journey.

  • Collect Ocean Debris

    Match and remove harmful objects like plastic bottles, fishing nets, and scattered waste before they overwhelm the board.

  • Restore Coral Reefs

    Use precise matches and special items to heal damaged coral patches and bring color back to underwater ecosystems.

  • Clear Hazard Zones

    Break through blocked spaces, remove pollution clusters, and reclaim areas hidden beneath layers of damage.

  • Protect Fragile Sea Life

    Complete mission goals while keeping the environment stable and creating space for marine creatures to return.
